Xavier High School:

Even though Xavier High School was an all boys school, I can honestly say that my time there was time well spent. I transferred out of the East Hampton public schools system to attend Xavier after middle school. In 2017 I graduated from Xavier after four years of hard work and determination. I can honestly say that Xavier surely prepared me for the struggles of college and gave me a solid foundation for a successful education.

University of Maine:

I chose UMaine because it was a perfect fit for me. And so far that statement holds true. I haven't one complaint with the school so far. Two main reasons that I chose this school are 1. Because of the great business school and academics and 2. I love the area that surrounds my campus. The state of Maine has always been one of my favorite places to travel to since I was very young. Being able to attend school here is quite a privilege.
